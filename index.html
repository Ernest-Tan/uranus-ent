About Us

Welcome to Uranus Enterprise, a dynamic company founded in the vibrant community of Penang’s Chung Ling High School. Specializing in cutting-edge STEM-related products, we strive to inspire innovation, curiosity, and progress while staying deeply committed to conservating and protective mother Earth. Guided by our core values of teamwork, creativity, integrity, and sustainability, we aim to empower future generations to explore new frontiers while protecting our planet.

Inspired by the vast wonders of the galaxy, our name reflects our mission: to reach for the stars, embrace infinite possibilities, and create solutions that leave a positive impact on Earth. At Uranus Enterprise, we believe that by uniting passion with purpose, we can shape a brighter and more sustainable future for all.

Uranus Enterprise consists of 35 dedicated students and 3 experienced teachers, with the goal of innovate and create together. Each member is selectively chosen through a rigorous interview process, ensuring that their unique skills and expertise align with their assigned roles. By leveraging individual strengths, we operate efficiently and professionally, striving for excellence in every project we undertake.
So far, we have successfully organized 3 sales events, providing our members with hands-on experience in marketing, customer engagement, and business operations. These events serve as valuable learning opportunities, allowing students to develop essential skills such as communication, sales strategy, and teamwork. Through real-world practice, our team gains confidence and expertise, preparing them for future entrepreneurial and professional endeavors.

Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)

The Young Entrepreneurship and Upcycling session at SJKC Union was a remarkable event aimed at inspiring students to embrace sustainable business practices. Organized by CLHS Uranus Enterprise, the session highlighted the transformation of waste into innovative products while educating students on fundamental entrepreneurship concepts. Through interactive discussions and creative activities, participants explored the importance of environmental responsibility and business development. The event successfully fostered creativity and entrepreneurial thinking among young minds, encouraging them to become future changemakers. CLHS Uranus Enterprise expressed gratitude to SJKC Union for hosting and emphasized the importance of building a greener, more innovative future together.
